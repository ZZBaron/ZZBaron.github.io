Research Work

RF/Quantum Device Engineering Research

At the Laboratory of Physical Sciences (LPS), I conducted research on silicon quantum devices, focusing on RF reflectometry measurements of quantum dots. This work involved developing custom Python-based instrument measurement software using QCoDeS for various quantum computing equipment, including magnet controllers and frequency generators. The research utilized state-of-the-art equipment such as Helium-3 refrigerators and vacuum chambers for quantum device experiments.
