Supersymmetry Research

Project Overview

Working with Dr. Sylvester Gates' research team at the University of Maryland, I explored advanced theoretical physics concepts and contributed to cutting-edge research in supersymmetry theory. This ongoing research has been a significant part of my academic journey since Summer 2021.

Research Areas

  • Advanced theoretical physics investigations focusing on supersymmetry
  • In-depth study of gauge theory and its applications
  • Analysis of group representation theory
  • Research into Poincare algebra and its implications
  • Study of Adinkra symbols and their applications in supersymmetry

Technical Focus

The research delved into several advanced mathematical and theoretical concepts:

  • Analysis of Adinkra symbols and their mathematical properties
  • Investigation of supermultiples and their conserved supercurrents
  • Study of Kähler geometry and its applications in supersymmetry
  • Application of quantum field theory principles
  • Mathematical modeling of supersymmetric systems

Research Impact

This work has contributed to:

  • Advanced understanding of supersymmetry theory
  • Development of new mathematical frameworks for analyzing supersymmetric systems
  • Enhanced comprehension of the relationship between Adinkra symbols and physical systems
  • Exploration of fundamental theoretical physics principles

The research, conducted under the guidance of Dr. S. James Gates Jr., has implications for our understanding of fundamental physics and the mathematical structures underlying supersymmetry theory.
